french-inspired, northwest ingredient-focused fare

At La Fenetre we embrace the french cuisine with a northwest flare. La fenetre means window and in our minds food can be that window opening you up to another world. Chef Jacques Lemerre and Melanie Cousteau create weekly menus that reflect their love for the seasonal flavors of Oregon as well as the french countryside. The freshest local produce,organic, pasture-raised meats from our favorite farms are thoughtfully paired with outstanding wines and delicate cocktails to please and delight the senses. A meal at La Fenetre is an intimate experience.Our wine and cocktail lists take their cues from the kitchen and are tastefully selected by wine director Lara Lee Mosley. We look forward to serving you soon.
